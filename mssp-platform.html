No cross-tenant visibility

MSSPs had to log into each client separately to see alerts. No way to get a unified picture across all managed accounts.

Manual context-switching between tenants kills analyst productivity. Every extra client multiplies the overhead.

No way to control which MSSP analysts can access which client tenants. No role mapping, no audit trail.

02 // the solution

A Unified Command Center for MSSPs

Cross-tenant SOC view

See alerts, investigations, and metrics from ALL client tenants in one dashboard. Real-time aggregation across schemas.

Account switcher

Seamlessly switch between client contexts. Run reruns, view details, all from the MSSP dashboard. Zero page reloads.

Delegated access control

MSSP roles mapped per-tenant. Analysts only see clients they're assigned to. Full audit trail on every action.

One pane of glass for security teams managing dozens - or hundreds - of client environments.

03 // under the hood

Cross-Schema SQL That Actually Scales

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . MSSP Tenant (schema: tenant_mssp) . . | . . |--- UNION ALL ---> tenant_client_1.findings . . | tenant_client_2.findings . . | tenant_client_3.findings . . | ... . . v . . Unified Dashboard (paginated, sorted, filtered) . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

Cross-schema UNION

SQL-level UNION ALL across tenant schemas instead of application-level merge. Database does the heavy lifting.

Partition aggregation

Window functions and partition-based aggregation to eliminate N+1 subqueries. Single pass over the unified result set.

Session-based access

Child tenant access via server sessions, not JWT. Simpler auth flow, no token bloat, easier to revoke.

04 // feature highlights

From Zero to Full MSSP in 11 Months

MSSP Workspaces

Hierarchical tenant management. Parent-child relationships with configurable access levels per analyst.

God Mode

Internal tool for Simbian team to access any child account for debugging. Separate from customer MSSP access.

SAML Role Mapping

MSSP roles synced from identity provider. Different roles per child tenant via SAML assertions.

Performance

Optimized aggregation queries. MSSP pagination across 10+ schemas in <500ms. No analyst ever waits.
